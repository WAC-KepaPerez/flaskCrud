About FlaskCrud App

FlaskCrud is a user-friendly Flask application designed to streamline workout tracking. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast, athlete, or simply someone looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle, FlaskCrud simplifies the process of recording and monitoring your workouts. With intuitive features and easy setup, managing your exercise routine has never been more convenient.
